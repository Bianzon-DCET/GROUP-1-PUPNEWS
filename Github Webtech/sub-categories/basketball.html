PUP Basketball Team Wins Championship

Published on: January 22, 2025

The PUP basketball team celebrated their championship win with a thrilling performance that left fans in awe. The game was a nail-biter until the very end, with both teams giving their all. The final score of 89-81 reflected the intense competition and the determination of the players.
